Business Description

Win80x Technologies Private Limited operates a skill-based online gaming platform that offers quiz-based games where users can participate using tokens and win rewards based on their performance and knowledge.

Our Services Include:
  • Online Quiz Gaming Platform
  • Skill-Based Gaming Competitions
  • Real Money Rewards System
  • Secure Payment Processing
  • User Wallet Management
  • 24/7 Customer Support
Revenue Model:

Our platform operates on a commission-based model where we retain a percentage of the total gaming pool for platform maintenance, development, taxes, and operations, while distributing the majority to winners based on their performance.

Target Market:

We cater to adult users (18+ years) across India who enjoy skill-based gaming and quiz competitions. Our platform promotes responsible gaming and educational content.
